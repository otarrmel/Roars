Getafe

Getafe (also spelt Jetafe) is a 3rd municipal income class municipality in the province of Bohol, Philippines. According to the 2015 census, it has a population of 30,955. In the 2016 election, it had 18,576 registered voters.

2013 Earthquake Effect

Getafe and surrounding areas suffered moderate-to-severe damage to houses, churches, and other structures in the 2013 Bohol earthquake, which caused approximately 130 fatalities in total.

Election 2016

In the 2016 election, it had 18,576 registered voters, meaning that 60% of the population are aged 18 and over.

Jetafe

An Getafe, na inaapod man na Jetafe, sarong ikaapat na klaseng banwaan kan probinsya nin Bohol, Filipinas. Susog sa senso kan 2007,. igwang 27,852 katawo na nageerok igdi.

Bayan ng Getafe

Ang Bayan ng Getafe o Jetafe ay isang Ika-4 na klaseng bayan sa lalawigan ng Bohol, Pilipinas. Ayon sa senso noong 2000, ito ay ma populasyon na 26,826 sa may 4,694 na kabahayan.
